Firstly, you need to know your semester syllabus so you can enter the credits of all subjects of your semester.

And then need to know how much grade points you can secure in semester subjects.

The CGPA is calculated based on the total credit points(credits x Grade points) of all subjects divided by the total credits of all subjects.
